## Verified Browser Findings

3. JPMorgan Chase Institute, "Understanding the use of AI among small businesses" (April 14, 2026). Verified findings: AI adoption among small businesses increased steadily from 2019 through 2025, with a notable acceleration beginning around 2023. New businesses in the 2025 cohort reached 10% AI adoption in about six months, compared with more than six years for the 2019 cohort. The report also states that employer firms adopted AI at nearly twice the rate of nonemployer firms, and knowledge-intensive industries such as information, professional services, and educational services led adoption. This strongly supports a category-based approach for professional services, education, consulting, marketing, finance, legal support, and other knowledge-heavy industries.
